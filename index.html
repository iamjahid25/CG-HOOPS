About Us

CG HOOPS

Having played at the VJBL, Australian Schools Nationals (ASC), BIG V and AAU Tournaments, I have been put through many elite training sessions and academies over my playing career

I have been coached by those at the state, national and international level. Combining all my knowledge and experience, CG HOOPS is an elite basketball training service that looks to develop athletes to a new level.

“I want my athletes to train not like the average athlete does. With Hunger and Determination. With resilience in all circumstances. More importantly, to carry these traits and mentality of training to all aspects of life.”
